Welcome to Hi Dictionary

This dictionary app is a powerful tool that provides the meaning, examples of usage, synonyms, and antonyms of any word you search for. Users simply need to type a word, and the app instantly displays its details. If the word is not available, the app shows an error message along with a retry option.Additionally, the app includes a light and dark mode toggle to enhance user experience and accessibility.
